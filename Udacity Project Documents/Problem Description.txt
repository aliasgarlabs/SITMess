Menu for the meal in the mess is always a reason of curiosity to students. 
It makes them choose whether to have meal in the mess or go somewhere else 
if that day's menu is not of their taste. 


With Symbi Mess App:
-User can browse upto Sunday's menu of a week
-Like the menu
-RSVP (Lot's of food is wasted when a lot of students go outside for meals. 
Especially during weekends. This will help the cooking staff to only prepare food accordingly.)

-Share the day's menu
-Get notified when the mess starts serving a meal
-Shows current meal menu in the notification.]
